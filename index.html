Plutox is not a chat bot. It is a structured AI assistant designed to produce usable outputs, not conversations.

Who is this for?

  • Builders who have ideas but need a concrete plan
  • Professionals who want answers in a fixed structure
  • People tired of vague, generic AI replies

How Plutox works

Instead of open-ended chat, Plutox uses predefined questions to guide your thinking.

  • You answer a small set of focused questions
  • The agent follows a fixed reasoning framework
  • You receive a clear, opinionated output

What you get

  • A concrete action plan
  • Clear assumptions and constraints
  • No fluff, no generic advice
