Plutox

A system for freezing hard decisions.

Plutox is not a chat bot. It is a decision framework designed to turn uncertainty into a clear commitment — with trade-offs made explicit.

What’s on your mind?

Just write everything down. Don’t organize it. Don’t decide yet.

I prefer the structured form →
You’re not making a decision yet. Next step: Plutox will organize what you wrote, then you can freeze it.

The real problem

Most people don’t lack information. They lack the ability to freeze a decision and accept its consequences.

Endless thinking feels safe — but it delays action.

How Decision Freezer works

  • Start with a free-write brain dump (no structure required)
  • Plutox organizes it into a clean decision shape
  • Then it freezes one decision with explicit trade-offs

What you get

  • A frozen decision
  • The assumptions behind it
  • What you are explicitly choosing to give up
  • A concrete 7-day plan
