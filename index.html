Plutox is not a chat bot. It is a decision framework designed to turn uncertainty into a clear commitment — with trade-offs made explicit.

The real problem

Most people don’t lack information. They lack the ability to freeze a decision and accept its consequences.

Endless thinking feels safe — but it delays action.

How Decision Freezer works

  • A small set of structured questions
  • Explicit assumptions and constraints
  • A single decision with clear trade-offs

What you get

  • A frozen decision
  • The assumptions behind it
  • What you are explicitly choosing to give up
